First-Gen Wolverines!

Welcome, First-Gen Students!

Roughly 4,000 students at the University of Michigan are the first in their families to attend college. While this achievement is something to be proud of, it can also feel overwhelming at times.

Fortunately, U-M offers a wealth of resources and a strong support network to help first-generation students succeed. This portal will guide you to helpful programs, communities, and opportunities built to support you.

The First-Gen Gateway is your go-to portal for mentorship programs, networking, and advocacy for First-Gen students at U-M.

Connect with peers through FGENSO, a student-led group that promotes awareness and support for First-Gen students.

M-Connect Program

This community helps community college transfer students transition smoothly into life at Michigan.

Mentorship Opportunities

Get paired with faculty, staff, or student mentors who can help guide you through your academic and personal journey.
